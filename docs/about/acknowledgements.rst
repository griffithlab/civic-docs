Acknowledgements
================
First and foremost we are grateful to the community of curators, editors, domain experts, and users who make CIViC possible.

Funding
-------

The CIViC project and ClinGen Somatic Cancer Biomedical Knowledgebase are currently supported by the National Institutes of Health (NIH) National Cancer Institute (NCI) under Award Numbers U24CA305456, U24CA275783, and R50CA305824, including a funding supplement from the NIH Office of Data Science Strategy (ODSS). CIViC is also supported by the Washington University Institute of Clinical and Translational Sciences grant UL1TR002345 from the National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences (NCATS) of the NIH. The content of this website is solely the responsibility of the creators and does not necessarily represent the official views of the NIH or other funding sources.
